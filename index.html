Retrieval augmented generation, in plain terms

RAG connects a language model to your own documents. Ask a question, and the system first retrieves the relevant records, then answers from them and shows where the answer came from.

  • Grounded answers. Responses come from your documents, not from whatever the model half-remembers.
  • Fewer fabrications. Because every answer cites its source, a wrong one is easy to catch.
  • Fully private. The whole pipeline, retrieval and generation, runs on your own hardware.
