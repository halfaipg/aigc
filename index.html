What is RAG?

Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is an AI framework that combines the power of large language models with the ability to retrieve and reference specific information from your business data.

The Benefits of RAG

  • Enhanced Accuracy: Ground AI responses in factual, up-to-date information from your trusted sources.
  • Reduced Hallucinations: Minimize the risk of AI generating incorrect or fabricated information.
  • Data Privacy: Keep your sensitive information secure while still leveraging AI capabilities.
  • Customized Context: AI responses that understand your specific business terminology and requirements.
