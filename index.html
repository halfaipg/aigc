What we do

Six things, done end to end. Every one of them can run entirely inside your own network.

AI agents and automations

Agents that take real work off your team's plate: intake, follow-ups, reporting, and the repetitive steps between your systems.

Voice agents and AI receptionists

Voice AI that answers your phones, schedules appointments, and routes callers, around the clock, in your business's own voice.

AI pipelines and data structuring

Ingest pipelines that turn messy files, scans, and exports into structured, context-rich data your systems and models can use.

Knowledge and retrieval systems

Language models grounded in your own documents, so answers come with sources instead of guesses.

Local models and fine-tuning

Open models tuned on your domain's language and tasks, running on local GPUs. You own the weights and everything they learned.

Strategy and IT enablement

Straight answers about where AI pays off in your business and where it doesn't, working alongside your IT team, not around it.

Your data stays under your control

Public cloud AI means sending your records to someone else's shared servers. We take the opposite approach: local models on dedicated GPUs, in your building or in our Cleveland datacenter, behind access rules you set.

  • Local models on dedicated GPUs
  • Air-gapped deployment options
  • You own the models and the weights
  • No public cloud, no shared tenancy

Enterprise GPUs, without the capital expense

We run enterprise GPU servers in our Cleveland-area datacenter and rent them to businesses that want serious AI capacity without buying it. Hopper and Blackwell class hardware, provisioned for your workloads, with our engineers consulting your IT team on how to put it to work.

  • Your models, your data. Private tenancy on dedicated hardware. Nothing shared, nothing sent to a public cloud.
  • Local and reachable. Your compute lives in the Cleveland area, run by the engineers you can actually call.
  • Rent or own. Start on our hardware, and if owning makes sense later, we spec and build the same stack in your racks.

How an engagement runs

  1. 1

    Discovery and assessment

    We look at your workflows, systems, and data and identify where AI would actually earn its keep.

    We map the integration, structure the data it needs, and decide where each piece runs: our datacenter or your hardware.

    You get a working proof of concept built on your real data and workflows, so you can judge results, not promises.

  4. 4

    Deployment and integration

    We put the production system on the right hardware, wire it into the tools your team already uses, and stay with your IT team until it runs without us.
